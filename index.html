Fontys DEV

Fontys University of Applied Sciences is one of the largest universities in the Netherlands, offering a wide range of bachelor's and master's programs across various fields such as technology, business, health, and social sciences. With a strong emphasis on practical learning and international collaboration, Fontys prepares students for the global job market. The university is known for its innovative teaching methods, state-of-the-art facilities, and a vibrant student community that fosters personal and professional growth. Fontys also values research and development, contributing to advancements in various industries while maintaining a close connection with local businesses and organizations.
